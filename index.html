Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle service for a unit in a location with a locked gate, like a construction site?

We coordinate directly with the site manager. You can provide us with a key or gate code, or we can schedule our service visits for a time when personnel will be on-site to grant access. We are very flexible in working within your security protocols.

What happens if it's extremely hot or cold? Does that affect the units?

Our standard units are designed to function well in all weather. The chemicals we use are formulated to control odor effectively even in high heat. For extreme cold, our luxury restroom trailers are fully heated, providing a warm and comfortable experience for your guests.

Can I request a specific delivery time, like exactly at 9:00 AM?

While we guarantee delivery on your requested date, we typically provide a delivery "window" (e.g., between 8 AM and 12 PM) to account for traffic and routing logistics. We will always do our best to accommodate specific time requests whenever possible.

What is the most sanitary option you offer?

While all our units are delivered in a sanitized condition, the most hygienic setup combines a flushing toilet unit with a standalone handwashing station. Our luxury trailers offer the highest level of sanitation, with flushing toilets and sinks with running water in each stall.

How much space do I need to clear for a luxury restroom trailer delivery?

A luxury trailer requires significant clearance. You will need a wide, clear path (at least 12 feet wide) with no low-hanging branches or wires (at least 14 feet of vertical clearance). The ground must be firm and level. We will consult with you to assess the site prior to delivery.
